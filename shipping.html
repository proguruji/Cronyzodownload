Clear policy for wholesale orders, tracking and delivery OTP system.

Our Shipping Promise

Cronyzo handles shipment for all orders. Wholesalers do not dispatch directly — this ensures consistent tracking, verified OTP handovers and better buyer protection.

  • Processing time: 1–2 business days after order confirmation.
  • Delivery time: Pan-India 3–10 business days depending on location.
  • Courier partners: We use trusted partners and own local stores for last-mile handover.
  • Shipping charges: Calculated at checkout by weight & zone.
  • Cancellation: Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours. After that, contact support.

OTP Delivery Flow

  1. Wholesaler hands package to Cronyzo shipper — Shipper confirms pickup with OTP-WH.
  2. Item arrives at local store near buyer — Store manager confirms intake with OTP.
  3. Delivery boy delivers to buyer — Buyer confirms delivery with final OTP (only final OTP fully visible).

All intermediate OTPs are masked in user UI except final delivery OTP for security.

Returns & Damages

Defective items can be returned within 7 days. Contact support with order ID and photos. Return pickup will be arranged by Cronyzo.
